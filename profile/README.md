## Statuses
Idea - Purely the thought of a datapack that seems reasonable enough to do with datapacks

Planning - A datapack that's preparing for development by solidifying documentation and processes

Developing - A datapack that is in the process of being developed

Finalizing - A datapack that has been fully developed and is preparing for release

Released - A datapack that has been finished and released. This does not mean it's feature complete

Feature Complete - A datapack that has had all of its original ideas added to it

Archived - A datapack that has stopped receiving updates, but may receive them in the future

Abandoned - A datapack that will no longer receive updates

Hold - A datapack that began development, but stopped unexpectedly. Likely to receive updates in the future
